Introduction

Greetings, to my visitor

One day I was traveling to a very popular destination in Indonesia known as Bali Island. Finally, after a very long time nearly 14 years I can go back to come to this amazing place. At the first touch, my plan was just to visit some famous "Tourist Attraction" and then go back to jakarta, I've planned it will be just for 14 days estimated. Following the plans that i've made and doing all my plans to visit places like Tanah Lot Temple, Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary in Ubud, the so quite beach in Karangasem known as Virgin Beach, Nusa Dua, Seminyak, yet of course Kuta Beach. I feel like it wasn't enough, like something is missing so I decided to extend my stay in Bali. And, here's the excitement starts..
